Wembeerly IPA

England supporters group, Block 109, has launched a beer in partnership with Portobello Brewery, which they’re selling to raise money for Grassroots for Good, a football charity which supports the grassroots game.

Wembeerly IPA, which already has orders from 15 pubs around the capital, is made using the exact same rye grass seeds as the hallowed turf at Wembley, making it the first beer in the world to be made from a football pitch.

With Wembley playing host to all three of England’s group games, plus the semi-final and final, Kunal Sapat, Founder of Block 109, is hoping to get it stocked in enough pubs and bars to make it the beer of choice for every discerning football fan this summer.

To make the project a reality, Block 109 pooled the resources of its members and teamed up with master brewers at Portobello Brewing Company, who turned their football dreams into a hoppy reality
