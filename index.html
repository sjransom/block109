Wembeerly IPA

To celebrate Block 109’s growth, we’ve decided to brew our own beer. But in our typical unique style, we chose to create a drink with a difference.

So we’ve launched a beer in partnership with Portobello Brewery, which we’re selling to raise money for grassroots fooball.

With Wembley playing host to all three of England’s group games, plus the semi-final and final, we’re hoping to get it stocked in enough pubs and bars to make it the beer of choice for every discerning football fan this summer.

Every time a football fan buys a bottle of Wembeerly IPA they’ll be donating to grassroots causes such Grassroots For Good, a community network started by Hackney Wick FC that champions the work of socially conscious amateur football clubs tackling inequality and lack of investment.

Portobello Brewing

To make the project a reality, Block 109 pooled the resources of its members and teamed up with the master brewers at Portobello Brewing Company, who turned our football dreams into a hoppy reality.
